FAQ

Common Questions

Can I join the class online?
Yes! All classes have a live online option via Zoom. You can attend from anywhere in Sri Lanka or abroad without being physically present at Colors Institute.
Can I watch class recordings if I miss a session?
Absolutely. All sessions are recorded and uploaded to the BS KING LMS portal within 24 hours. You can rewatch them unlimited times throughout the year.
How long is the full theory course?
The full theory program runs approximately 12 months, covering all syllabus units in depth. Revision and paper class programs are available as separate short-term modules.
Do I get printed notes?
Yes. All enrolled students receive comprehensive printed smart notes for each unit. Digital versions are also available on the LMS for download and mobile access.
Can I pay the class fee monthly?
Yes, monthly payment plans are available. Please contact Sir via WhatsApp on 071 785 9629 to discuss your enrollment and payment schedule.
What subjects does the Business Studies class cover?
The class covers the complete A/L Business Studies syllabus including business concepts, management principles, marketing, finance, accounting fundamentals, and the external business environment.
