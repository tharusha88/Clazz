The Syllabus Journey

A structured progression to master the A/L Physics syllabus.

Mechanics

Laying the foundation. Vectors, kinematics, dynamics, statics, and circular motion. The backbone of A/L Physics.

Understanding SHM, wave properties, sound, and light. Building analytical skills for wave phenomena.

Fields & Electricity

Gravitational, Electric, and Magnetic fields followed by Current Electricity. The highest weighted section in the paper.
